Frequently Asked Questions

 

How does AI Market Cap find stocks to trade?
We scan All AI and AI-niche companies daily and pull those with earnings reports coming in the next 1-14 days. Each stock is scored based on institutional analyst ratings, buy percentage, 5-day upside potential, and strong buy count. The highest-scored stocks closest to earnings are highlighted as AI's Suggested Trade.
What does the "Score" mean?
The composite score ranges from 0-100+. It is calculated from: Analyst count (30pts) + Buy% (30pts) + 5-Day upside target (20pts) + Strong buy ratings (2pts each, max 20). A score of 80+ is our Strong Buy threshold — these are the stocks with the most institutional backing and momentum.
What are PE Target, 3D Momentum, and 5D Momentum?
All three are derived from ATM options straddles at the time of the scan. PE Target (conservative) assumes the straddle moves at face value. 3D Momentum is straddle × 3 — a mid-range target. 5D Momentum is straddle × 5 — maximum upside if the stock moves big after earnings. Use these to set your limit sell based on your risk tolerance.
Is this a buy-and-hold strategy?
No. This is a pre-earnings momentum strategy. You enter 1-14 days before earnings and exit 1-5 days after a positive earnings report. We rotate to the next top pick after each trade — it's a continuous cycle, not a long-term hold.
Can I cancel anytime?
